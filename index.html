// SURVIVAL GUIDE

PRO STRATEGIES

Essential tips to push past your limits and survive the full 120 seconds.

01

Keep Moving

Standing still is a death sentence. Constant movement makes you harder to hit and lets you kite enemies into kill zones.

02

Prioritize Threats

Runners first — they're fast and flanking. Then Splitters before they multiply. Elites last (but don't ignore them).

03

Build Synergy

Multi-Shot + Spread Shot = bullet hell. Damage + Fire Rate = boss melter. Speed + Orbit Shield = untouchable.

04

Collect XP Orbs

Green orbs give XP. Level up faster = more upgrades = more power. Don't leave XP on the ground.

05

Save Your Health

HP pickups heal 20 HP. Don't rush into danger to grab them — clear enemies first, then collect.

06

Boss Strategy

Bosses spawn every ~60 seconds. Use open space. Never stop moving. Stack damage upgrades before the boss wave.

07

Combo Chain

Kills within 2.5 seconds build your combo meter. Higher combos mean more style points — and bragging rights.

08

Weapon Mastery

Each weapon excels in different situations. Spread = crowds. Sniper = elites. Laser = sustained DPS. Rockets = AoE.
