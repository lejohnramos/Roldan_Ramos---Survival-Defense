# 🧟 Survive the Horde

A brutal browser‑based survival shooter. Waves of enemies keep coming – upgrade your weapon, master your movement, and survive as long as you can. Play solo or team up with up to 4 friends in multiplayer.

## 🎯 Game Features

- **Auto‑targeting combat** – Focus on movement; bullets lock onto the nearest enemy.
- **11+ upgrades** – Increase damage, fire rate, multi‑shot, or unlock devastating new weapons (Spread Shot, Sniper, Laser, Rocket, Orbit Shield).
- **Escalating waves** – Every 15 seconds the wave counter increases, spawning faster and tougher enemies.
- **Boss every 60 seconds** – Massive enemy with 500+ HP and screen‑shake entrance.
- **Combo system** – Chain kills within 2.5 seconds to build your combo meter.
- **Permanent shop** – Spend diarite to increase max HP, fire rate, or unlock starting weapons.
- **Multiplayer (2‑4 players)** – Survive together via WebSocket.

## 🕹️ Controls (Single Player)

| Action          | Keyboard / Mouse            |
|-----------------|-----------------------------|
| Move            | `WASD` or `Arrow Keys`      |
| Auto‑fire       | Enabled by default (shoots nearest enemy) |
| Toggle manual fire | Press `F` – then **Left Click** to shoot |
| Pause           | `ESC` or `P`                 |

> On mobile, a virtual joystick appears on the left side of the screen.

### Single Player
1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser.
2. Click **START GAME**.
